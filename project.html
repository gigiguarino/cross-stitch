Image to Cross-stitch Pattern Converter

To convert an image to a cross-stitch pattern, you need to input the link to the image, the maximum height and width of your desired cross-stitch, and the count of the fabric you are using. To avoid distortion of your image, we will scale your image to fit inside your desired height and width.





Inputs


  • A link to your image (http://www)
  • The maximum height and width of your desired cross-stitch, in inches
  • Your fabric count (stitches/inch)
  • The number of different colors you want in your cross-stitch. Please only values in between 1 and 20
